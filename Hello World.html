The Knowledge Virus

Scenario

Fleshcasters have had advanced technology for ten million years. They have mastered time, space, Artificial Intelligence, Biologic Programming, Nano-tech and things that are beyond human comprihension.

  • Time
  • Space
  • AI
  • Biologic Programming
  • Nano-tech
  • Unimaginable others

The arrow of time travels in a single direction. As the universe expands, time unfolds. The Fleshcasters are able to compress space, there by reversing the direction that time flows in.

The Fleshcasters have found a way to fold the endless three dimentional plane in a direction of their choice using strange macines with many sharp edges that seem to produce gravitational pull. They also have control over the strength and weakness of said gravity, allowing them to bend and warp space.

Artificial Inteligence is a way to produce hand made genius. Many humans who are so called "masters" of AI could not comprehend the wisdom and power in the Fleshcasters Intelligence. They have created beings that not only know many facts such as the definition of otorhinolaryngologist, but they know how to come up with their own ideas and techniques such as what not to do during an otorhinolaryngology surgery.

The Fleshcasters have become their own creators through biologic programming, and by creating the instructions of their own phinotypical existence. They are able to play biology like a harp, they've created new forms and overcome all natural limitations, to become gods in their own universal biome.

Nano-Technology is about programming reality at the subatomic scale. The Fleshcasters are able to code symbolic systems using quarks, leptons, nutrinos, and of course, the all important, higsboson (a.k.a the god partical). The machinery at this scale is able to peirce the very veil of earthbound dimentions, bringing forth information, knowledge, and wisdom that is otherwise unknowable.

If you were able to understand the horrifying technology in this category, immediate eviceration would ensue.
